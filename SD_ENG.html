    It was my turn to escape from the windy Bay Area to warm SoCal to visit MC!

    Almost every time when I met friends or teachers from San Diego
    and asked for their comments on hometown,
    they would give a bunch of praise on SD.
    Now I finally got to see this city,
    a place constantly being named as “the best city in the world”…

    Hotel Recommended: Sheraton San Diego Hotel & Marina
    It is located on a small man-made peninsula near San Diego airport and
    served as such a great place for vacation. Right beside the seashore,
    you can see gorgeous view of shining water or sunset from room’s balcony and
    try some aquatic sports through the free tickets given by the hotel.
    The price is not very friendly though, so it might not be a good choice for those who will stay for a while.
